How to use
1.Click Activate or press Alt+G
2.Hover over any image on the page
3.Click the image to lock it, then use Copy URL, Copy Image, or Download
4.Press Esc or click to unlock, then Esc again to exit
🔒 Lock mode
Click an image to lock onto it. While locked: click anywhere or press Esc to unlock. Press Esc again to exit the grabber entirely.
